Monthly Q&A Blog Post

Do you have a question you want to ask me about privacy and/or security that you think others might benefit from knowing, too? Here's your chance! On the first Saturday of every month, I will be posting a reader Q&A blog post. This will consist of 2-5 questions (depending on how many I receive and the complexity of the questions).

Notes

  • All information will be keptin the strictest confidence.
  • The inbox used is ProtonMail, but it does pass through SimpleLogin. If you want to email directly via CTemplar, Proton, or Tutantoa you may, but please put "Public Q&A" in the subject line so I know your question is intended to be shared publicly. See the Contact Page for additional ways to contact me and PGP keys.
  • I reserve the right to not select your question for public answering. If I feel the question warrants a more discreet response, I may respond.
  • I may also contact you for additional information or context to ensure I answer the question properly. Again, this information will be held in confidence.
