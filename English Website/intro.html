Introduction: Section Introduction

I have divided this site up into standalone sections, but there are a few concepts that are universal and critical to the world of privacy and security. The next few pages of this site are dedicated to explain basic concepts that will come up over and over again throughout this site, such as how surveillance works, how most networked digital communications work, and what "open source" means (and why it matters). If you feel like you sufficiently understand these topics, you can feel free to skip this section, or you can come back to the pages as you need.
