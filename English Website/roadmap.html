Current Roadmap

The following is a list of upcoming projects that The New OIl is working on. If you want to support us to make any of these projects possible, then please consider donating to The New Oil to help finance these projects. Keep in mind that these projects may be implemented earlier than expected or pushed back depending on funding.

2022: January-June

  • Un-released Podcast: We are very excited about this project and don't want to give too much away. The only thing we can say is that we're working on a highly-produced, highly-scripted podcast evolving around real world developments in the privacy community. We hope to release one season of ten episodes each year, but that will largely depend on how much material we can gather. At the very least it will be a one-season miniseries.
  • Site translation: We plan to translate the website into multiple languages, beginning with a couple of big languages. We would like to continue to expand this project. We plan to use GitLab to help us maintain the translated versions of the website so that readers will be able to help post corrections and discuss this project.

2022: July-December

  • Merch store: As part of our continued plans to monetize, we plant to open a merch store to sell merchandise to those who want to support us. In order to do this in the most privacy respecting way possible, we will need to make it to where we will involve as minimal third parties as possible (meaning that we will self-host the shopping frunt and use platform such as "WooCommerce"" or "OpenCart" to do ths). We will also be try to pre-order the merchandise so that it doesn't have to be made on-demand. This will both lower costs for The New Oil and will allow us to provide our users with as much privacy as possible, though going this route will mean that this will be an additional expense for us when the time comes.

2023 And Beyond

The following are projects we plan to implement, however at this time will not be possible an is our lowest priority due to the lack of demand and support for those projects.

  • Nextcloud Instance: We plan to someday be able to provide a publicy-available Nextcloud Instance to help those in need, take back their privacy and reduce their dependency on services from big tech companies like Google, Microsoft and Amazon, etc for example. We plan to and would like to run this Nextcloud Instance on a freemium based model with space for everyone who needs it. We also plan to make it as ptivate as possible by enable E2EE (end-to-end encryption) on our Instance and pick a privacy fouseded hosting provider in a jurisdiction with as good parivcy wals and regulations (and no, we are not planning to host it in the 14 eyes).
  • Matrix Instance: The default Matrix Instance (which our rooms are curantly part of and that most of our team members use) has some of the most terrible privacy out there and chose to implement CloudFlare (a non privacy focused cyber security company) to help defend against DoS and DDoS attacks. While we do understand this decision and don't fault them for it, we plan to use our own Instance instead and would like to provide our future users with the up most privacy and secuirty as possible. So we would to start our own instance free of CloudFlare and locaded in a jurisdiction with a privacy fouseded hosting provider that is free of the 14 eyes.
